Our Story

Why We Built This Hostel

In 2022, we saw a clear gap in the Rau-Indore market. Medicaps University and surrounding colleges were attracting thousands of students every admission season — but the available hostels were either far from campus, poorly maintained, or designed without genuine concern for student wellbeing.

Radiance Residency was built to be different. We chose a plot directly across from the Medicaps University main gate — a 2-minute walk. We designed five room categories so students from different budgets could find their fit. We built in CCTV, security guards, and direct parent communication from day one. We serve home-style meals included in the fee — not as an extra. We promise 24-48 hour resolution on any service issue.

Four years in, we have hosted 112+ residents. We are listed on Wikidata (Q139378489), Justdial, MagicBricks, and The Trip Guide. We have never had a significant safety incident. We are not the biggest hostel in Indore — by design. We are the most premium hostel in Rau, designed for parents who want one less thing to worry about.
