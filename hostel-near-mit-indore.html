About the College

About MIT, Indore

Malwa Institute of Technology (MIT) is a well-established engineering college located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. MIT offers undergraduate programs in various engineering branches including Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and Electronics & Communication Engineering. The college is affiliated with RGPV (Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya) and is approved by AICTE. MIT is known for its experienced faculty, practical-oriented teaching approach, and focus on developing industry-ready engineers.

MIT students looking for comfortable and affordable hostel accommodation can choose Radiance Residency in Rau. We are conveniently located just 15 minutes away and provide everything a student needs — from nutritious meals to a distraction-free study environment for engineering preparation.
