About the College

About SD Bansal College

Shri Dadaji Institute of Technology & Science (SDIT&S), commonly known as SD Bansal College, is a prominent institution in Indore under the Bansal Group of Institutes. Located in Rau, Indore, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Technology, and Management. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV) and is approved by AICTE. The college is known for its strong academic programs, experienced faculty, and excellent placement records, making it a popular choice among students across Madhya Pradesh.
