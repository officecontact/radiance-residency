IPS Academy Indore — Admission, Courses, Fees & Student Life (2026)

February 28, 2026 9 min read

IPS Academy is one of the most well-known and established private educational institutions in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Founded in 1999 by the Indore Professional Studies (IPS) Group, the academy has grown into a multi-disciplinary campus offering programs across engineering, management, pharmacy, law, science, commerce, and humanities. If you are considering IPS Academy for your higher education, this comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know in 2026.

About IPS Academy

IPS Academy is located on the AB Road (Indore-Dhar Highway) in the Rajendra Nagar area of Indore. The campus is spread across a large area and houses multiple institutes under one umbrella. The institution is affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) and is approved by AICTE, BCI, PCI, and other regulatory bodies depending on the specific program.

The academy has earned a strong reputation over the past two decades for its academic rigour, experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and consistent placement records. It attracts students from all across Madhya Pradesh and neighbouring states like Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Chhattisgarh.

Courses Offered at IPS Academy

IPS Academy offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs through its various constituent institutes:

Institute of Engineering & Science (IES)

  • B.E. in Computer Science, IT, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics & Communication, Electrical
  • M.E. in select specializations
  • Admission through JEE Main counselling and MP DTE

School of Management (IPSOM)

  • MBA with specializations in Finance, Marketing, HR, IT
  • BBA, BCA programs
  • Admission through MAT/CAT/CMAT scores or DAVV CET

Institute of Pharmacy

  • B.Pharm, D.Pharm
  • M.Pharm in various specializations

School of Law

  • BA LLB (5-year integrated program)
  • LLB (3-year program)
  • LLM postgraduate program

Other Programs

  • B.Sc., M.Sc. in various science streams
  • B.Com, M.Com
  • B.Ed, M.Ed (Education)
  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des)

Admission Process (2026)

The admission process at IPS Academy varies by program. For engineering programs (B.E.), admissions are primarily through JEE Main scores followed by MP DTE counselling. Management programs (MBA) accept scores from CAT, MAT, CMAT, or the DAVV CET entrance exam. Law admissions may require CLAT scores or the DAVV CET.

For undergraduate programs like BBA, BCA, B.Sc., and B.Com, admissions are generally merit-based through 12th board exam scores with direct application to the college. The admission window typically opens between May and July each year. It is advisable to visit the official IPS Academy website or contact their admission office for the latest deadlines and requirements.

Fee Structure (Approximate)

The following are approximate annual fee ranges for various programs at IPS Academy. Actual fees may vary and should be confirmed directly with the institution:

  • B.E. (Engineering): 70,000 to 1,10,000 rupees per year (varies by branch and category)
  • MBA: 80,000 to 1,20,000 rupees per year
  • BBA/BCA: 40,000 to 60,000 rupees per year
  • B.Pharm: 70,000 to 90,000 rupees per year
  • BA LLB: 50,000 to 80,000 rupees per year
  • B.Sc./B.Com: 25,000 to 45,000 rupees per year

Scholarships and fee waivers are available for meritorious students and those from economically weaker sections. The institution participates in government scholarship schemes as well.

Placements & Career Prospects

IPS Academy has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell that works throughout the year to connect students with recruiters. The engineering and management streams have traditionally seen the strongest placement numbers. Companies that have recruited from IPS Academy include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, HCL, Capgemini, and several other IT and consulting firms.

For engineering students, the average placement package typically ranges from 3 to 5 lakh rupees per annum, with top performers securing packages of 8 to 12 lakh rupees. MBA placements see similar ranges, with the best offers going to students with strong academics and internship experience. The placement cell also organizes pre-placement training, mock interviews, aptitude workshops, and resume-building sessions.

Beyond campus placements, many IPS Academy alumni have gone on to pursue higher education at institutions like IIMs, IITs, and universities abroad. The strong alumni network provides mentoring and referral opportunities for current students.

Campus Life & Facilities

The IPS Academy campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a central library with digital resources, computer centres, seminar halls, and auditoriums. The campus also has sports facilities including cricket grounds, basketball courts, and indoor sports areas.

Student life at IPS Academy is vibrant with various clubs, committees, and cultural events. The annual technical and cultural festivals attract participation from colleges across Madhya Pradesh. Students can participate in coding competitions, debates, music, dance, dramatics, and sports tournaments throughout the academic year.

The campus has a canteen serving affordable meals and snacks. There are ATMs and stationery shops on or near campus. The surrounding area has developed significantly, with numerous food joints, cafes, and essential services within easy reach of students.

Accommodation Options for IPS Academy Students

Many students at IPS Academy come from outside Indore and need reliable accommodation. While the college does not have extensive on-campus hostel facilities, there are several PGs and hostels in the nearby areas. Students typically look for accommodation in Rau, Pigdamber, and surrounding localities which offer affordable options with easy access to the campus.

When choosing accommodation near IPS Academy, students should prioritize proximity, meal plans, safety, and value for money. Living in a well-managed hostel that provides meals and Wi-Fi can significantly enhance the student experience by eliminating daily hassles and allowing more time for academics and extracurriculars.
