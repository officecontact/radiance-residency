SD Bansal College Indore — Courses, Fees, Placements & Review (2026)

Everything you need to know about SD Bansal College — from admissions to placements, campus life to nearby hostels.

March 10, 2026 10 min read

If you are considering pursuing engineering, pharmacy, or management studies in Indore, SD Bansal College (officially known as Shri Dadaji Institute of Technology & Science, or SDITS) is likely on your radar. Located in the Rau area of Indore, this college has been a popular choice among students from across Madhya Pradesh and neighboring states for over two decades.

In this detailed review, we cover everything about SD Bansal College — its courses, fee structure, placement record, faculty quality, campus infrastructure, pros and cons, and where to stay if you get admitted here.

College Overview

SD Bansal College was established in 1999 and is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal. The college is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and is located on the Indore-Dewas Bypass Road near Rau. The campus is spread over a sizeable area with separate blocks for engineering, pharmacy, and management departments.

Quick Facts:
Established: 1999 | Affiliated to: RGPV Bhopal | Approved by: AICTE
Location: Rau-Pithampur Road, Rau, Indore | Type: Private
Campus Size: ~10 acres | Total Students: ~2,000+

Courses Offered at SD Bansal College

SD Bansal College offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across engineering, pharmacy, and management. Here is the complete list of courses:

B.E. / B.Tech Programs (4 years):

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI&DS) — Newly Added

Pharmacy Programs:

  • B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) — 4 years
  • D.Pharm (Diploma in Pharmacy) — 2 years

Management Programs:

  • MBA (Master of Business Administration) — 2 years
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) — 3 years

Postgraduate Programs: M.Tech in select branches and M.Pharm are also available for students looking to pursue higher studies.

Fee Structure (2026-27)

SD Bansal College offers competitive fees compared to many private engineering colleges in Indore. Here is an approximate fee structure (subject to change — always confirm with the college directly):

Course Annual Fee (Approx.) Duration
B.E. / B.Tech Rs 55,000 - 75,000 4 years
B.Pharm Rs 60,000 - 80,000 4 years
D.Pharm Rs 45,000 - 55,000 2 years
MBA Rs 50,000 - 70,000 2 years
BBA Rs 35,000 - 45,000 3 years

Note: Fees vary based on category (general/OBC/SC/ST), admission through counselling vs. management quota, and scholarship eligibility. Government scholarship students may pay significantly less.

Placement Record & Companies

SD Bansal College has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell that organizes campus drives throughout the year. While the placement rate varies by branch and year, CSE and IT branches generally see the best placement percentages. Here is what you can expect:

  • Average Package: Rs 3 - 4.5 LPA
  • Highest Package (Recent): Rs 8 - 12 LPA (CSE/IT)
  • Top Recruiters: TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, Cognizant, HCL, Capgemini, Accenture
  • Other Recruiters: Byju's, Jaro Education, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, various startups
  • Placement Rate: Approximately 50-65% overall (higher for CSE/IT at 70-80%)

Students who actively participate in coding competitions, build projects, and do internships during their college years tend to land better packages. The college also encourages students to pursue certifications and skill-building beyond the curriculum.

Faculty & Campus Infrastructure

The faculty at SD Bansal College is a mix of experienced professors and younger teaching staff. Many faculty members hold M.Tech and PhD degrees. The quality of teaching is generally considered above average, especially in the CSE, IT, and Pharmacy departments.

Campus facilities include:

  • Well-equipped computer labs with high-speed internet
  • Separate labs for each engineering and pharmacy department
  • Central library with digital resources and reading rooms
  • Canteen with affordable meals
  • Sports ground for cricket, football, basketball, and volleyball
  • Seminar halls and auditorium for events and guest lectures
  • Wi-Fi-enabled campus

Pros & Cons — Honest Assessment

Pros:

  • Affordable fee structure compared to other private colleges in Indore
  • Good placement support for CSE and IT branches
  • Convenient location in Rau with good hostel options nearby
  • Active college events — tech fests, cultural events, and sports
  • Pharmacy department has a strong reputation
  • RGPV affiliation ensures recognized degree value

Cons:

  • Placement opportunities for non-CSE branches could be stronger
  • Campus size is moderate — not as large as some university campuses
  • No on-campus hostel facility (students need to find nearby PG/hostels)
  • Some students feel more industry exposure and workshops are needed
