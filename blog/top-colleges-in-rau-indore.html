Top 10 Colleges in Rau, Indore — Complete Guide for Students (2026)

March 15, 2026 10 min read

Rau, a fast-growing suburb of Indore in Madhya Pradesh, has emerged as one of central India's most prominent education hubs. With multiple universities, engineering colleges, and management institutes within a compact area, Rau attracts thousands of students from across the country every year. If you are planning to pursue higher education in Indore, this comprehensive guide to the top colleges in Rau will help you make an informed decision about your academic future.

1. Medicaps University

Medicaps University (formerly Medi-Caps Institute of Technology and Management) is a UGC-recognized private university and one of the most reputed institutions in the Rau-Indore belt. Established in 2000 and granted university status in 2014, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech (CSE, IT, ME, EC, Civil), MBA, BBA, BCA, M.Tech, MCA, B.Sc, M.Sc, B.Pharma, B.Com
  • Fee Range: Rs 70,000 - Rs 1,50,000 per year depending on the course
  • Placements: Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini, and Tech Mahindra. The average placement package for B.Tech CSE students is around Rs 4-6 LPA, with the highest going up to Rs 12-15 LPA.
  • Campus: Spread across a 50+ acre campus with modern labs, library, sports facilities, and auditorium

Medicaps is particularly strong in its engineering and computer science programs, and its proximity to the IT corridor of Indore gives students excellent industry exposure.

2. IPS Academy

IPS Academy is one of the largest and most well-known educational groups in Indore with multiple institutes offering courses in engineering, management, pharmacy, law, and more. The Institute of Engineering and Science (IES) under IPS Academy is NAAC-accredited and highly regarded.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, B.Pharma, M.Pharma, BBA, BCA, B.Com, BA LLB, LLM, B.Ed, D.Pharma
  • Fee Range: Rs 50,000 - Rs 1,30,000 per year
  • Placements: Strong placement cell with recruiters like Infosys, TCS, Amazon, Deloitte. Average packages range from Rs 3.5-5 LPA.
  • Campus: Well-maintained campus near Rajendra Nagar with excellent infrastructure

3. SD Bansal College of Technology

Shri Dadaji Institute of Technology & Science (SD Bansal) is an AICTE-approved engineering college located on the Indore-Ahmedabad highway, very close to Rau. It is known for its affordable fee structure and decent placement record.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech (CSE, ME, EC, Civil, EE), M.Tech, MBA, MCA
  • Fee Range: Rs 45,000 - Rs 90,000 per year
  • Placements: Companies like TCS, Infosys, and L&T recruit from campus. Average packages around Rs 3-4 LPA.
  • Campus: Located just minutes from Medicaps University, making it convenient for students staying in the Rau area

4. IIST (International Institute of Science & Technology)

IIST Indore is an AICTE-approved private engineering college with a growing reputation for quality education. Located on the Indore-Ahmedabad highway near Rau, it has been steadily improving its academic and placement performance.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech (CSE, IT, ME, EC, Civil), MBA, BBA, BCA
  • Fee Range: Rs 50,000 - Rs 1,00,000 per year
  • Placements: Growing placement statistics with companies like TCS, Infosys, and regional IT firms. Average packages around Rs 3-4.5 LPA.
  • Campus: Modern campus with well-equipped laboratories and a good library

5. Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research

Acropolis is an NAAC-accredited institution known for its strong focus on research and innovation. Located on the Bypass Road in Indore, it is a popular choice among engineering aspirants in the region.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech (CSE, ME, EC, Civil, IT), M.Tech, MBA
  • Fee Range: Rs 60,000 - Rs 1,10,000 per year
  • Placements: Strong placement cell with an average package of Rs 4-5 LPA. Top recruiters include TCS, Cognizant, Infosys, and Wipro.
  • Campus: Sprawling campus with excellent sports and cultural facilities

6. Prestige Institute of Engineering, Management & Research

Prestige Institute is a well-established college in Indore known for both its engineering and management programs. It has UGC recognition and is affiliated with RGPV Bhopal.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, BBA, BCA, MCA
  • Fee Range: Rs 55,000 - Rs 1,20,000 per year
  • Placements: Decent placement record with companies like Infosys, TCS, and Persistent. Average package around Rs 3.5-5 LPA.

7. Chameli Devi Group of Institutions

Chameli Devi offers a range of technical and professional courses. The engineering institute is AICTE-approved and affiliated to RGPV, offering quality education at competitive fees.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, B.Pharma, Diploma
  • Fee Range: Rs 40,000 - Rs 90,000 per year
  • Placements: Average package of Rs 3-4 LPA with companies like TCS, Wipro, and Infosys

8. Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya

Shri Vaishnav is a well-established university in Indore offering a wide range of programs from engineering to arts. The Shri Vaishnav Institute of Technology and Science (SVITS) is particularly popular among engineering students.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, MCA, B.Pharma, BA, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc
  • Fee Range: Rs 50,000 - Rs 1,20,000 per year
  • Placements: Good placement record with average packages of Rs 3.5-5 LPA

9. Oriental Institute of Science & Technology

Oriental Institute is an AICTE-approved college in Bhopal Road, Indore, offering engineering and management programs. It is affiliated with RGPV and has a growing reputation for academic quality.

  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, M.Tech
  • Fee Range: Rs 45,000 - Rs 1,00,000 per year
  • Placements: Average packages of Rs 3-4 LPA with growing recruiter presence

10. IIM Indore

While not in Rau itself, IIM Indore is located nearby and is one of India's premier management institutes. Established in 1996, it consistently ranks among the top 10 B-schools in the country and attracts top talent from across India.

  • Courses Offered: PGP (MBA equivalent), IPM (Integrated Programme in Management), EFPM, PhD
  • Fee Range: Rs 17-23 Lakhs for the complete PGP program
  • Placements: Average placement package of Rs 25-30 LPA with the highest going above Rs 50 LPA. Top recruiters include McKinsey, BCG, Goldman Sachs, Amazon, and Google.
  • Campus: World-class campus spread across 193 acres with state-of-the-art facilities

How to Choose the Right College

When selecting a college in the Rau-Indore area, consider these key factors:

  • Accreditation: Check for NAAC, NBA, and UGC recognition
  • Placement Record: Look at average and median packages, not just the highest
  • Faculty Quality: Research faculty credentials and student-teacher ratios
  • Infrastructure: Visit campuses to assess labs, libraries, and facilities
  • Student Accommodation: Ensure affordable and quality hostels or PGs are available nearby
  • Location & Connectivity: Consider proximity to public transport, markets, and hospitals
