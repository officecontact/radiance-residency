The 7 Pillars

How We Keep Students Safe

1. 24/7 CCTV Coverage

HD cameras at every entry, corridor, parking, and common area. Recordings retained for parent review. Monitored on-site by duty staff.

2. Security Guards

Trained guards stationed at main entry in rotating shifts. Visitor verification and entry logging at all times.

3. Controlled Entry

Visitors must register with valid government ID. Female visitors restricted to common areas. No overnight visitors. All entries logged.

4. Fire Safety

Fire extinguishers on every floor (serviced annually). Smoke detectors in common areas. Clearly marked emergency exits. No-smoking policy enforced.

5. Medical Access

First-aid kits on every floor. Bombay Hospital & Apollo Indore within 15 minutes. Manager trained in basic first aid. Transport assistance available.

6. Direct Parent Line

Parents WhatsApp/call the manager directly anytime. Welcome calls on arrival. Quarterly satisfaction check-ins. Open invitation to visit.

7. Digital Safety

Separate guest and resident Wi-Fi networks. Password rotation. Content-filter optional. Privacy-respecting policies for resident data.
