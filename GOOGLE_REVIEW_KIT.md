## Best practices
- **Ask at a happy moment** — after a good meal, festival, fee-renewal, or when a parent visits and is pleased.
- **Personalise the name** — generic blasts get ignored.
- **Don't incentivise** (no "review for discount") — against Google policy, risks removal.
- **Reply to every review** (you can, as owner) — Google rewards engagement; thank positives, address negatives calmly.
- **Cadence:** send to ~5–10 current students/parents per week, not all at once (a sudden spike looks unnatural).
- Aim for a steady climb to **20+ reviews** — that's where the local-pack needle really moves.
