Coming to Indore for Work? Here's Where to Stay

If you're relocating to Indore for a job at Siemens, L&T, Eaton, or Johnson Controls in Pithampur — or interning at a hospital like Bombay Hospital or Medanta, or joining a tech company like TCS or Infosys — the biggest unknown is: where exactly should I stay?

The answer is Rau. Most people from outside Indore haven't heard of it — but it's the suburb that sits right between Indore city and Pithampur on the highway, making it the perfect base for anyone working in the area.

📍 What and where is Rau?

Rau is a residential suburb on the Indore–Pithampur highway, about 15 km from Indore city centre and 10-15 km from Pithampur industrial area. It has a major university (Medicaps), multiple schools, markets, restaurants, pharmacies, and ATMs. It's a well-established, safe area — and significantly cheaper than staying in central Indore for the same quality.

Why Rau is the best base for Pithampur workers and Indore newcomers:
  • ✅ Closest residential area to Pithampur — 10-15 min commute, no city traffic
  • ✅ Much cheaper than Indore city centre — same quality of living, lower rent
  • ✅ On the highway — direct, smooth route to Pithampur, no detours or congestion
  • ✅ Active suburb — restaurants, shops, ATMs, pharmacy, petrol pump all within 1 km
  • ✅ Safe and established — major university campus right next door
  • ✅ Flexible short-term stays available — 1 to 3 months, no long lease needed
