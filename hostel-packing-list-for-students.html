FAQ

Packing Questions

What documents do I need for hostel admission?

Admission/offer letter, 10th and 12th marksheets (originals + 5 photocopies each), Aadhaar card (original + copies), Transfer Certificate, Migration Certificate, 20 passport-size photos, and any category/scholarship certificates.

Does Radiance Residency provide meals and bedding?

Yes. Home-style vegetarian meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) are included in rent. Rooms are fully furnished with beds. You do not need to bring a mattress, cooking equipment, or kitchen supplies.

How many clothes should I pack for hostel?

Pack for 7 days — that's enough for one laundry cycle. 5–7 T-shirts, 3–4 jeans/trousers, 1–2 formals, 7–10 sets of undergarments. Don't over-pack; you can always buy more locally in Indore.

Should I bring textbooks from home?

No. Wait until you arrive. Senior students sell second-hand textbooks at 50–70% off during the first week. Most reference materials are also available in the college library or online.

What medicines should I carry for hostel?

Paracetamol, stomach upset medicine (Digene/Pudin Hara), cold and cough medicine, basic first-aid kit, ORS sachets, and any prescription medicines you take regularly. A pharmacy is within a short distance of Rau area.
