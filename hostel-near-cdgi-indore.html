About the College

About Chameli Devi Group of Institutions

Chameli Devi Group of Institutions (CDGI), Indore is a well-known educational group offering programs in Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, and Applied Sciences. Affiliated to RGPV (Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya) and approved by AICTE, CDGI is recognized for its quality technical education, experienced faculty, modern labs, and consistent placement record. The institute attracts students from across Madhya Pradesh and beyond who seek practical, industry-oriented education in the heart of Indore.

Radiance Residency provides comfortable and affordable hostel accommodation for CDGI students who prefer off-campus living with all the conveniences of home.
