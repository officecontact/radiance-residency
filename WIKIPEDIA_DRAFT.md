'''Radiance Residency''' is a [[boarding house|boys hostel]] and [[paying guest]] accommodation facility located in Pigdamber, [[Rau, Indore|Rau]], [[Indore]], [[Madhya Pradesh]], India. Established in 2022, the hostel primarily serves students from nearby educational institutions including [[Medi-Caps University]], [[IPS Academy, Indore|IPS Academy]], the International Institute of Science and Technology (IIST Indore), and SD Bansal College of Engineering.

The facility is registered as [[Wikidata]] entity {{Wikidata|Q139378489}} and listed on the Indian business directories Justdial and MagicBricks.

== Location ==

Radiance Residency is situated directly opposite the main gate of Medi-Caps University in the Pigdamber neighbourhood of Rau, an outlying suburb southwest of Indore city. The location places the hostel within walking distance of several engineering, medical, and management institutions that form part of the Rau-Indore education cluster.<ref name="medicaps">{{Cite web |title=Medi-Caps University, Indore — Campus Location |url=https://www.medicaps.ac.in/ |access-date=2026-05-20}}</ref>

== History ==

The hostel was established in 2022 to serve the growing demand for student accommodation in the Rau-Indore area following expansion of nearby educational institutions in the 2010s and 2020s.<ref name="indore-edu-hub">{{Cite web |title=Indore — Educational institutions |url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Education_in_Indore |access-date=2026-05-20}}</ref>

== Facility ==

The hostel comprises five room categories — Standard, Superior, Superior (Air Conditioned), Deluxe, and Premium — across approximately 64 beds, available in single and double occupancy configurations.<ref name="justdial">{{Cite web |title=Radiance Residency Hostel — Justdial listing |url=https://www.justdial.com/Indore/Radiance-Residency-Hostel-In-Front-Of-Medicaps-University-Rau/0731PX731-X731-241211200908-I4F7_BZDET |website=Justdial |access-date=2026-05-20}}</ref>

Common facilities include vegetarian meals included in the monthly fee, 24-hour CCTV surveillance, security personnel, on-premises kitchen, study areas, parking, and a small mandir (prayer space).

## Additional sources to gather BEFORE submitting

Wikipedia's notability requires multiple independent sources. The draft uses 3 — try to add 2-3 more before submission:

2. **University accommodation listing** — If Medicaps or IIST website lists nearby accommodation options (even informally on a "for students" page), screenshot and archive on web.archive.org → cite the archive URL.

3. **Industry publication** — Indian Hostel Association directories, NoBroker corporate pages featuring hostels, etc.

4. **Government registry** — Any GST registration listing, local Indore Municipal Corporation business registry. Cite as primary source.

5. **Existing Wikidata-linked references** — If Q139378489 has any properties with reference URLs, those count.

Each additional source increases acceptance probability significantly. Aim for **at least 5 references** in the final article.
