About AC Rooms

Why Choose an AC Hostel in Indore?

Indore is known for its extreme summers, with temperatures often soaring above 40°C from April through June. For students who need to study, attend online classes, and prepare for exams, the heat can be a major distraction. An AC hostel room ensures you stay comfortable and focused no matter the weather. At Radiance Residency, our AC rooms come equipped with modern split air conditioners and are priced at just ₹9,000/month — with meals, Wi-Fi, electricity (including AC usage), housekeeping, and all amenities included. There are no separate electricity bills or hidden charges. For students on a tighter budget, our Non-AC rooms at ₹7,000/month offer the same great amenities without air conditioning. Located right in front of Medicaps University in Rau, we are also near IPS Academy, IIST, SD Bansal College, and IIM Indore, making us the ideal AC hostel choice for students across the region.
