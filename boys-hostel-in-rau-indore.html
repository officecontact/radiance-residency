Rau is one of the most prominent educational zones in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Home to prestigious institutions like Medicaps University, IPS Academy, IIST (International Institute of Science & Technology), SD Bansal College, and in close proximity to IIM Indore, Rau has become the go-to destination for thousands of students from across the country.
