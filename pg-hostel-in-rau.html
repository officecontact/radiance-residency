Frequently Asked Questions

What is a PG hostel in Rau, Indore?

A PG hostel combines the private-room setup of a paying guest arrangement with full hostel-level amenities — meals, security, WiFi, and housekeeping all managed for you. Radiance Residency is the most premium PG hostel in Rau, directly in front of Medicaps University.

What's the difference between a PG and a hostel?

A PG (Paying Guest) is typically a room in a private house with optional food and minimal management. A hostel is a purpose-built facility with structured meals, 24/7 security, and professional management. Radiance Residency is a managed PG hostel — you get a private room with attached bathroom, plus all hostel services.
