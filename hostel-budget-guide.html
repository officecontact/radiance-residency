💰 10 Money-Saving Tips

1️⃣ Eat at Hostel Mess

Fresh, prepared meals are included. Eating outside = ₹200-400/day extra. Save ₹6,000-12,000/month by eating at hostel.

2️⃣ Use Hostel Wi-Fi

At Radiance: 50+ Mbps included. Don't buy separate data plans. Save ₹1,000-2,000/month on unnecessary internet.

3️⃣ Share Transport Costs

Travel with roommates or classmates. Share auto costs. Save ₹200-500/month on commute.

4️⃣ Avoid Cafe Culture

₹100 coffee 2-3x/day = ₹6,000-9,000/month. Make tea/coffee in hostel. Save ₹5,000+/month.

5️⃣ Buy in Bulk

Shampoo, toiletries, snacks — buy in bulk with roommates. Save 20-30% on essentials.

6️⃣ Limit Entertainment

Movies, outings, shopping add up. Plan to ₹500-800/month on entertainment, not ₹2,000+.

7️⃣ Use Hostel Laundry

Many hostels have laundry facilities. Don't send clothes to expensive laundromats. Save ₹200-400/month.

8️⃣ Plan Monthly Budget

Track spending weekly. Know where money goes. Budgeting saves ₹500-1,000/month automatically.

9️⃣ Walk When Possible

Radiance is 2 min from Medicaps, near shops. Walk instead of auto for short distances. Save ₹300-500/month.

🔟 Get Student Discounts

College ID = discounts at restaurants, cinemas, shops. Save ₹500-1,000/month on entertainment.
